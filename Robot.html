Mechanical Marauders

Our Robot!

Our robot is a collection of many different systems we have seen other teams using. The original designers had success with their design we decided to benchmark on it and make it better. One
such example of this is our collection system, we saw a video on the FTC subreddit of a team completing the snake cipher in 72 seconds and decided this would be a good design to use on our
robot. Here our robots various systems will be explained.

Drive Train

The drive train we decided to go with this year was a 6 wheel drive system using 2 omni wheels and a green grippy wheel on each side. The omni wheels provide us great mobilty while the grippy
wheels provide us with stablity in order to climb the balancing stone. one thing we did differently with out drive train that other teams havent done is using only one motor on each side. This
decreases the speed but also lowers the weight and allows us to not have bulky motors in the way of crucial parts.
